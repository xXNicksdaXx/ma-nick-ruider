This chapter presents the conceptual design of a graphical editor for managing \gls{uvl}-based feature models and its extension to contextual behavioral programs.
It outlines the core architectural decisions, the integration points for Behavioral Programming concepts, and the runtime synchronization mechanisms that enable consistent \textit{models@run.time} views and user-driven modifications.
